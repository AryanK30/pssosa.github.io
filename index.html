WELCOME TO THE PATENTIA SECONDARY SCHOOL OLD STUDENTS' ASSOCIATION WEBSITE

Mission

Our mission is to collaborate with the Patentia Secondary School through events, activities and otherwise in other provide support such as financially, to the students of the school to remedy any inconveniences they may currently be faced with.

Goals
  • To ensure that all students get quality education
  • To ensure that the institution at which students learn is safe and encouraging
  • To provide facilities so that students have the best possible learning experiences
  • To promote positive messages to students and teachers alike so that we can foster an environment that is worth being
  • To give students access to opportunites that they have been taught would not be available to them because of school placement
